Next-Gen UX

SmartAgeri is a 3D immersive platform that transforms crop planning into a visual experience.
Soil health, rainfall patterns, and climate conditions appear as interactive layers on a 3D district map, allowing users to explore and understand their environment through motion, depth, and color.
Designed for intuitive decision-making, AgriX makes complex agricultural data feel natural, visual, and engaging.
